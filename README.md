# 🏗️ CEMEX — Cement Exchange System

## 📌 Project Overview

**CEMEX (Cement Exchange System)** is an innovative platform designed to solve major challenges in the cement market in Ethiopia.  

The system enables **direct interaction between cement factories and consumers**, reducing intermediaries, lowering inflated prices, and improving transparency in the supply chain.  

It also supports **government regulation and taxation**, helping to control and monitor the cement market more effectively.

---

## 🌍 Problem Statement

Through research and over **20 interviews with cement buyers and sellers**, we identified key issues in the cement market:

- High and unstable cement prices  
- Multiple intermediaries increasing costs  
- Difficulty in government taxation and regulation  
- Limited access to cement information for rural users  

---

## 💡 Our Solution

CEMEX introduces a **technology-driven supply chain system** with three main components:

### 1️⃣ Factory Application  
- Desktop application for cement factories  
- Allows factories to manage and sell cement directly  
- Source code:  
  👉 https://github.com/suud-abrar/CEMEXFactory-App-

---

### 2️⃣ SMS-Based Ordering System  
- Enables users to purchase cement using **basic mobile phones (no internet required)**  
- Improves accessibility for people in rural and low-income areas  
- Users can place orders via SMS directly from their phones  

---

### 3️⃣ Government Monitoring System  
- Desktop application for government authorities  
- Helps monitor:
  - Cement distribution  
  - Pricing control  
  - Tax collection  
- Supports better regulation of the cement market
